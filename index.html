About Me

I am a passionate and results-driven Cyber Security Analyst with hands-on experience in Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT), Web Application Security, Network Security, and SOC Operations. I have successfully identified, analyzed, and helped remediate security vulnerabilities including SQL Injection (SQLi), Cross-Site Scripting (XSS), Insecure Direct Object Reference (IDOR), Broken Authentication, and other OWASP Top 10 risks using Burp Suite, Nmap, Nessus, Wireshark, Metasploit, OWASP ZAP, SQLMap, Trivy, Aircrack-ng, and Docker. Passionate about ethical hacking, threat detection, incident response, and continuous learning, I am committed to protecting digital assets and building secure, resilient systems.
